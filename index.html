Open since January 2018, and loving every minute of my Etsy experience! I am a recent graduate of Chestnut Hill College, where I got my Master's degree in Clinical and Counseling Psychology. I currently work as an outpatient therapist for children and adolescents and I am loving it! I love what I do in my Etsy shop too, is is the BEST side gig - it helps me to have a couple of extra bucks while I am paying off the student loans (LOLZ)!

I really enjoy making many random bracelets in tons of different colors and patterns! I make bracelets in between working my jobs, it helps keep me sane!

I LOVE making custom bracelets and getting to learn about the people who are ordering them. Message directly for custom orders! (Custom orders take up to 2 weeks to complete and must be purchased in full before I start making them).

***Suzanne's Strings only ships within the U.S. and EU currently***

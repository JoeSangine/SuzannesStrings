About Me!

Suzanne (owner) started making bracelets during summer camp days as a wee kiddo. From there, Suzanne would often make bracelets for friends and family members, eventually this passion grew. With encouragement from family and friends,

Suzanne started out selling a stock of bracelets on EBay in summer of 2017 which sold out by the end of the summer. Suzanne then decided to open an Etsy shop and has had over 5,000 and counting sales to date. During the COVID-19 pandemic Suzanne and family decided to use their artistic talents to make masks to donate to local organizations in the area. During this time, Suzanne really found a niche for sewing which then blossomed into many more options for the shop. Now, Suzanne is hoping to reach a larger scale of individuals through having a custom website.
